Practice Area

Media and Entertainment Law

At Atriad IP Law Associates, we work closely with artists, creators, and independent talent to help them navigate the legal side of their creative careers. Our focus is on protecting creative ownership, ensuring fair contractual terms, and supporting artists as their work moves from idea to public release.

The creative industry often involves multiple stakeholders - producers, platforms, agencies, brands, and collaborators. Without clear legal structuring, artists may unintentionally lose control over their work, rights, or future income. Our role is to help artists understand what they are signing, what rights they are giving away, and how their creative interests can be protected over time.

We approach media and entertainment law with sensitivity to the realities of creative work. Our advice is practical, transparent, and designed to empower artists to make informed decisions without feeling pressured or overwhelmed by legal language.

Our media and entertainment law services include:

  • Review and negotiation of artist, performer, and creator agreements
  • Drafting of contracts for music, film, digital content, and independent productions
  • Advisory on copyright ownership, authorship, and control of creative works
  • Licensing and monetization of creative content across platforms
  • Guidance on royalties, revenue sharing, and credit attribution
  • Brand endorsements, influencer collaborations, and commercial tie-ups
  • Assistance in resolving disputes related to content use or contractual obligations

Based in Mumbai, we support artists, musicians, filmmakers, writers, designers, and digital creators at various stages of their careers. Our objective is to create a legal foundation that allows creative work to grow securely, with rights respected and interests protected.
