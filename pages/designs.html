Practice Area

Designs

At Atriad IP Law Associates, we advise businesses on protecting the visual elements that give a product its identity and market presence. Design registration addresses the outward appearance of a product - its contours, surface patterns, structure, and visual composition - features that often influence consumer recognition and preference.

Design law does not concern itself with technical function, but with visual distinctiveness. Registration under the Designs Act, 2000 provides the registered proprietor with exclusive rights over the design for a given statutory period, enabling lawful control over its use and preventing imitation that dilutes originality.

Our work in design protection is deliberate and detail-driven. We assist clients in determining whether a design is capable of protection, guide them through the filing and examination process, and help maintain those rights as products evolve and expand into new markets. Where conflicts arise, we approach enforcement with a balanced understanding of both legal position and commercial impact.

Our design-related services include:

  • Evaluation of design eligibility prior to filing
  • Preparation and filing of design applications
  • Assistance with securing protection for designs in foreign jurisdictions
  • Handling examination reports and procedural objections
  • Advisory and representation in matters involving unauthorized imitation
  • Renewal tracking and long-term design portfolio support
  • Structuring and documenting design licensing and assignments

We work with designers, manufacturers, startups, and established enterprises to ensure that visual originality is not only created, but meaningfully protected and sustained.
