Practice Area

Trademarks

At Atriad IP Law Associates, we assist businesses and creators in protecting the identity they have carefully built. Our trademark services are designed to help you secure your brand name, logo, tagline, and other distinctive elements that set you apart in the marketplace.

We understand that a trademark is not merely a legal formality, but a reflection of the effort, creativity, and reputation behind a business. Our role is to guide you through the registration and protection process with clarity, transparency, and attention to detail, ensuring your rights are properly secured under Indian law and, where required, across international jurisdictions.

Our trademark-related services include:

  • Trademark searches, clearance checks, and risk assessment
  • Trademark filing and prosecution before the Indian Trade Marks Registry
  • Opposition, rectification, revocation, and related proceedings
  • Trademark monitoring, renewals, and post-registration compliance
  • International trademark filings through the Madrid Protocol and foreign affiliates
  • Opinions on infringement, validity, and registrability
  • Assistance in trademark enforcement, including civil and criminal remedies
  • Trademark assignment, licensing, and franchising support
  • Ongoing IP portfolio management

We work with clients across varied sectors, offering thoughtful and end-to-end trademark support that grows with your business. Whether you are launching a new brand or strengthening an existing one, our focus remains the same: to make trademark protection clear, accessible, and dependable.
